Really Good Films
(according to Uyen Dien)
There is nothing quite like the feeling of having just watched what will become one of your favorite films. It’s the same kind of feeling you get when you’re watching one of your favorite films with a friend who is seeing it for the first time. You want them to love it like you do; you want them to feel all the feels that you did when you first watched it. This website is built on this idea. It’s just as much about the love for sharing or discovering films as it is about watching them.
