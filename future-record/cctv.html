CCTV_Rec1
This is a visualisation of the pile of CCTV files that had not been used for at least weeks or years. Within these CCTV files each frame of footage amounts to something. In shopping centres, in dash cams, outside homes; CCTV is everywhere. How much data is it really taking up? How much of it is actually being used? Through these visualisations, experience a world that has been taken over by the CCTV Grey Goo.
